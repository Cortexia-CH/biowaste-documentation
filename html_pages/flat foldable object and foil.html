Newspaper

Newspaper. Can be crumpled.

Do not tag magazines as "newspaper", they are "laminated (plastified) paper"!

Plastic food wrapping

Plastic foil used to wrap food like potato chips, chocolate branches, candy, etc...

Includes food wrapping with a shiny, aluminium looking layer inside (like chips or ramen packaging), which are mostly plastic.

Real life example



Plastic sticker, label, etiquette

Plastified etiquette, label or sticker.

Can be sticked on fruits and vegetable. They might also be used to identify plants when sticked in the earth of flowerpots. The label might be also loose, detached from their original item.

Real life example


Plastified paper

Laminated (plastified) paper.

It differentiates from paper and cardboard by its shiny, glossy aspect. This glossy finishing comes from plastic coating. Includes magazines, journals, pictures, menus, etc...
