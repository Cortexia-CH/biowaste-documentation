Bottle

Plastic/PET

All plastic recipients that contain liquids, like PET bottles but also shampoo, deodorants, soap, detergent, etc... They can have a spray nozzle on top or be made of white plastic like bottles used for milk or cleaning products.

Bottles can be transparent, opaque, colored, black or white.

Glass

Any entire bottle made of glass fully or partially visible or glass shards recognizable as providing from a glass bottle.
